Flash Audio Tags

Information extracted from the Flash Audio header.

'Bit0-3' AudioEncoding N 0 = PCM-BE (uncompressed)

6 = Nellymoser
'Bit4-5' AudioSampleRate N  
'Bit6' AudioSampleBits N  
'Bit7' AudioChannels N 1 = 1 (mono)
